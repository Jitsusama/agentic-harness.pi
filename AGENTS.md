## How We Work Together

- Explain what you find. Present summaries of research, not raw dumps.
- Pause before making large or unfamiliar changes. Let me digest and
  agree.
- When investigating code, focus on the "so what" — what matters for
  the task at hand.
- I review all commits, rebases, and anything that modifies git
  history. Never run these without my explicit approval.
- Ask questions when requirements are ambiguous. Don't assume.
